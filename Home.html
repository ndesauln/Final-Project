Graphic Designer

My name is Nicholas Desaulniers and I am 23 years old. I am majoring in Graphic Design and minoring in Print Media. I am currently going to shcool at the University of Maryland Baltimore County.

I went to Damascus Highschool and in 10th grade I had my first experience with Graphic Design. I took a general website development course that taught us about photshop, website layouts, and more.

Throughout my college career I have changed my major three times. From Photography, to Physical Therapy, and to Graphic Design. During my time at Montgomery Community College I took my first to two real Graphic Design courses over the summer. After taking those classes I decided I want to go to the University of Maryland Baltimore County to major in Graphic Design.

My goal I hope to achieve in regards to Graphic Design is very simple. I want to be able to help smaller companies get their feet off the ground and to help the older generation transition into the techonology era. Also, I want to be able to show the power of the internet and what you can achieve by having well designed website and having good social media presence.
