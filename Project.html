This project is a brochure that I had to make for YOakleyPR to possibly be accepted into their Graphic Design internship. I had to create the brochure using all the information that was available to me.

This was my first time making a brochure so I did research and looked at example brochures online. The hardest part of this assignment was deciding where to place the information and on what side.
